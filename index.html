What's going on here?

How would you rather spend a Superbowl Sunday? Sitting on a couch or building applications that change the way people follows sports and interact with their favorite teams and fellow fans? We are organizing Sports Hack Day in Seattle during Superbowl weekend. We are gathering hackers, data crunchers and sports fans from across the Pacific northwest to converge on Seattle where we will hack on sports themed projects and then gather for a group viewing of the Super Bowl.
